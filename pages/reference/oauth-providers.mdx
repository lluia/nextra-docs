# OAuth Providers

Oauth providers allow you to add "Sign in with (Google, Facebook, etc...)" buttons in your application.

Your users are redirected to an external service (like Google) for authentication and come back to your application authenticated.

- [Follow our OAuth setup tutorial](/setup/oauth-authentication)
- [Learn more about OAuth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OAuth)

The following listed of official OAuth providers are created and maintained by our community:
